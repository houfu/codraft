## How It Works

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="1. Add Your Template" icon="document">
    Drop a Word, HTML, or Markdown file into `templates/`. Add `{{ placeholders }}` where content changes. That's a working template — no form builder, no configuration file.
  </Card>
  <Card title="2. Claude Interviews You" icon="puzzle">
    Tell Claude to "prepare a [document type]". Claude finds the template and walks you through a natural conversation — skipping irrelevant sections, letting you answer in any order, and collecting lists with an "add another?" flow.
  </Card>
  <Card title="3. Get Your Document" icon="approve-check">
    Confirm the collected values and the finished document is rendered to `output/` — Word, HTML, or Markdown with an optional PDF, plus a transcript of the entire interview.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## Features

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Talk, Don't Type" icon="comment">
    Claude interviews you through a conversation, not a form. Answer in any order, point to a photo instead of typing, change your mind mid-way. Ambiguity gets clarified, not rejected.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Templates You Already Have" icon="document">
    Use Word, HTML, or Markdown files — no proprietary format, no vendor lock-in. Add `{{ placeholders }}` where content changes. If you have existing documents, you may already have templates that work.
  </Card>
  <Card title="No Interview to Build" icon="star">
    In other tools, writing the template is half the work — then you build the form separately. Codraft reads your template and generates the interview automatically. Add a variable, the question appears. Remove it, the question disappears.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Your Files Stay Local" icon="laptop">
    Templates and output live on your machine. No cloud uploads, no SaaS account, no subscription. Share your setup with a colleague by zipping the folder.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Built-In Audit Trail" icon="open-book">
    Every session saves a human-readable transcript and a structured interview log alongside your document. See exactly what was asked, what was answered, and what ended up in the final output.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
