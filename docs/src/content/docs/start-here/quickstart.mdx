This guide walks you through preparing the bundled **Bonterms Mutual NDA** using Claude Cowork. You will go from zero to a completed Word document in under five minutes.

<Aside>
Codraft ships with the [Bonterms Mutual NDA](https://bonterms.com/) as an example template. It is a real, production-quality mutual NDA released under CC BY 4.0.
</Aside>

## Prepare the NDA

<Steps>

1. **Open Claude Cowork and attach your Codraft folder**

   Create a new project in Claude Cowork and select the folder where you extracted (or cloned) Codraft as the project directory. Cowork loads the skills and project context automatically.

2. **Ask Claude to prepare the NDA**

   Type a natural request:

   ```
   Prepare a mutual NDA
   ```

   Claude matches your request to the `Bonterms_Mutual_NDA` template, analyzes it for variables, and starts the interview.

3. **Answer the interview questions**

   Claude walks through the 13 variables in the template, grouping related fields together. Here is what a typical interview looks like:

   ```
   Let's prepare the Bonterms Mutual NDA. I'll walk through the details
   with you.

   First, what is the purpose of this NDA?
   > Evaluating a potential partnership between our two companies

   What is the effective date?
   > 2026-03-01

   What is the term of the NDA?
   > 2 years

   What is the confidentiality period?
   > 3 years

   What is the governing law?
   > State of California

   Which courts will have jurisdiction?
   > State and federal courts in San Francisco, California

   Are there any additional terms?
   > None

   Now for Party 1. What is Party 1's name?
   > Acme Corp

   Party 1 signatory name and title?
   > Jane Smith, CEO

   Party 1 signing date?
   > 2026-03-01

   And Party 2. What is Party 2's name?
   > Widgets Inc

   Party 2 signatory name and title?
   > Bob Johnson, General Counsel

   Party 2 signing date?
   > 2026-03-01
   ```

4. **Confirm the values**

   Claude presents a summary of all collected values and asks you to confirm before rendering:

   ```
   Here's a summary of the NDA details:

   Purpose: Evaluating a potential partnership between our two companies
   Effective Date: 2026-03-01
   Term: 2 years
   Confidentiality Period: 3 years
   Governing Law: State of California
   Courts: State and federal courts in San Francisco, California
   Additional Terms: None
   Party 1: Acme Corp (Jane Smith, CEO) — signing 2026-03-01
   Party 2: Widgets Inc (Bob Johnson, General Counsel) — signing 2026-03-01

   Does everything look correct?
   > Yes, go ahead
   ```

5. **Get the output file**

   Claude renders the template and saves the completed document to the `output/` folder:

   ```
   Done! Your NDA has been saved to:
   output/Bonterms_Mutual_NDA_Acme_Corp_2026-03-01/Bonterms-Mutual-NDA.docx
   ```

   Open the `.docx` file in Word, Google Docs, or any compatible application to review the final document.

</Steps>

## What just happened

Behind the scenes, Codraft ran three steps:

1. **Discovery** -- Claude matched "mutual NDA" to the `Bonterms_Mutual_NDA` template directory
2. **Analysis** -- The Analyzer skill parsed the `.docx` template, extracted all 13 variables, and produced a `manifest.yaml`
3. **Rendering** -- After you confirmed the values, the Renderer skill used `docxtpl` to fill in the template and saved the output
