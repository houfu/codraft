title: Questions Are Generated by AI
description: Claude analyzes your template and conducts a natural-language interview — no form builder required

Most document automation tools make you build a form before anyone can fill in a template. Codraft skips that step entirely. Claude reads your template and figures out what to ask.

## How it works

When you ask Codraft to prepare a document, the Analyzer skill scans the template for every `{{ variable }}` placeholder, every `{% if %}` condition, and every `{% for %}` loop. It produces a structured manifest that maps out exactly what information is needed.

Claude then uses that manifest to conduct a conversational interview. No form, no UI, no configuration required.

## A natural conversation

Instead of filling out a grid of labelled fields, you have a back-and-forth with Claude:

```
Claude: What is the client's full legal name?
You:    Acme Pte Ltd

Claude: What is the effective date for this agreement?
You:    March 1, 2026

Claude: Does this engagement include IP assignment?
You:    Yes

Claude: Who will own the assigned IP?
You:    Acme Pte Ltd
```

Related questions are grouped together. Conditional questions are skipped when they do not apply. Loop items are collected one at a time with a natural "add another?" prompt. The interview adapts to the template, not the other way around.

## Smart enough out of the box

Claude infers question types from variable names. A variable called `effective_date` gets asked as a date. One called `contact_email` gets email-style prompting. A variable starting with `include_` is treated as a yes/no question. You do not need to configure any of this.

## Fine-tune when you want to

For template authors who want more control, an optional `config.yaml` lets you customize the experience:

- Write your own question text
- Define choice lists (e.g., payment method: bank transfer, cheque, crypto)
- Group related variables into named sections
- Set default values
- Add cross-field validation rules

The configuration is entirely optional. Without it, Codraft still produces a complete, usable interview from the template alone.
